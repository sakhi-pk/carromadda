How to Play

🎯 Objective: Pocket all 9 of your colour coins before your opponent does.

⬅️ Position Striker: Drag the striker left/right along your baseline.

🎯 Aim: Touch anywhere on the board to set direction and charge power.

🚀 Shoot: Release to flick! Power bar shows shot strength.

👑 Queen (Red): Pocket it, then cover with your coin for +10 bonus!

⚠️ Foul: Striker goes in pocket = opponent gets 3 pts + your turn ends.

🏆 Win: First to pocket all 9 coins wins + earns bonus coins!
